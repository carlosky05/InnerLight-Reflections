Kristine Pelletier Garcia, LCSW

Psychotherapist & Grief Movement Guide

My name is Kristine Pelletier Garcia and I am a bilingual (English/Spanish) Licensed Clinical Social Worker with over 10 years of clinical experience. I provide online therapy services to adults in the state of North Carolina and I am the owner of InnerLight Reflections. My therapeutic approach is client-centered and adapted to the needs identified by each person. While I specialize in life changes, grief, and coping, I consider each client unique and special, and I take pride in offering attentive, focused, and compassionate listening to support them on their journey.

From an early age, I was drawn to listening attentively to others to better understand how they were feeling, especially during some of the most challenging moments in their lives. Change often brings a mix of emotions and thoughts that can manifest as anxiety, stress, or depression, disrupting personal life. My approach has helped clients find ways to move forward through the many life transitions they face.

I offer a compassionate and supportive approach to help clients navigate the challenges they are facing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is therapy 100% online?

Yes. No matter where you are in North Carolina, we meet via video call in a safe and private space.

What do I need for the session?

You only need a device with a camera and microphone, a good internet connection, and a quiet space where you feel comfortable.

How much does therapy cost?

The cost of therapy varies depending on your needs. While I do not accept insurance, I offer sliding scale rates when needed. Please contact me directly for more information.
