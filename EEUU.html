Frequently Asked Questions

Is therapy 100% online?

Yes. No matter where you are in North Carolina, we meet via video call in a safe and private space.

What do I need for the session?

You only need a device with a camera and microphone, a good internet connection, and a quiet space where you feel comfortable.

How much does therapy cost?

The cost of therapy varies depending on your needs. While I do not accept insurance, I offer sliding scale rates when needed. Please contact me directly for more information.
